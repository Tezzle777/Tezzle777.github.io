Recipe #1-Brownies

  • Time:35m
  • Serves:16-20
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetible Oil 3/4 Cups
Sugar 1 1/2 Cups
Vanilla Extract 1 1/2 tsp
Eggs 3
Flour 3/4 Cup
Cocoa 1/3 Cup + 2 1/2 tbsp
Baking Powder 1/4 tsp + 1/8 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9Ã—9 square baking pan, or line it with parchment paper.

Step 2: Mix together the oil, sugar and vanilla extract

Step 3: Add eggs and mix until well combined.

Step 4: Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt

Step 5: Slowly add egg to the mixture until well combined

Step 6: Pour the batter into the pan and spread evenly

Step 7: Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with a few crumbs

Recipe #2-Pancakes

  • Time:20m
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 1/2 Cups
Baking Powder 3 1/2 tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Sugar 1 tbsp
Milk 1 1/4 Cups
Egg 1
Butter 3 tbsp

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2:Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Source: Pancake recipe

Recipe #3-Waffles
  • Time:20m
  • Serves:2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Flour 2 Cups
Milk 1 3/4 Cups
Vegetable Oil 1/2 Cups
Sugar 1 tbsp
Vanilla Extract 1/2 tsp
Baking Powder 4 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp

Step 1: Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.

Step 2: Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot.

Source: Waffle recipe
